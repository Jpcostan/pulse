# Privacy Policy

**Pulsio — Turn Meetings Into Actions**

**Effective Date:** March 2026

---

Pulsio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All processing happens entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

Pulsio does not collect any personal information. No account creation is required to use the app.

The following data is created and stored locally on your iPhone only:

- Audio recordings of your meetings
- Transcripts generated by Apple's on-device Speech framework
- Action items detected by Apple's on-device NaturalLanguage framework
- Reminders and calendar events created through Apple's EventKit at your request

## In-App Purchases

Pulsio offers a one-time Pro upgrade through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Payments are handled entirely by Apple. Pulsio does not have access to users' payment information.

## Permissions

Pulsio requests the following permissions, each required for core functionality:

- **Microphone** — To record meeting audio
- **Speech Recognition** — To transcribe audio on-device
- **Reminders** — To create Apple Reminders from detected action items
- **Calendars** — To optionally create calendar events for action items with due dates

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time in Settings.

## Tracking

Pulsio does not track users. There are no analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party SDKs.

## Data Sharing

No user data is shared with third parties. No network requests are made except for In-App Purchase validation through Apple.

## Children's Privacy

Pulsio does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not collect information from any user.
